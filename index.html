About Me

My introduction

With almost 8 years of experience as a graphic designer since high school, I sought to overcome the stagnant point in my career and explore new opportunities. Motivated to develop myself further, I pursued self-study in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, focusing on becoming a frontend developer while leveraging my graphic design knowledge. During an internship, I joined a project that laid the foundation for Meella Company, applying for a frontend position to apply my newfound skills. Initially preferring to be a follower, I stepped up as the Lead Frontend Developer due to limited applicants. Despite initial doubts, a friend's advice on leadership reassured me that coordination and leveraging expertise were essential. With connections from previous work experience, I embraced the role, pushing my abilities to become a good Lead Frontend Developer. Subsequently, I secured a web developer position at Ogilvy and Mather Advertising, refining my frontend skills and gaining proficiency in CMS platforms and website analytics tools.
